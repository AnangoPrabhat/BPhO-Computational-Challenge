Privacy Policy

for Optics Simulator

Effective Date: 17th June 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Optics Simulator. This Privacy Policy outlines our firm commitment to protecting the privacy of our users. This policy applies to the Optics Simulator application (the "App"), developed by Anango Prabhat and Thales Swanson (the "Developers"). Our core privacy principle is simple: we do not collect, store, or process any of your personal data. Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be completely transparent about our data practices. The Optics Simulator app is designed to function without collecting any personally identifiable information or any other form of user data. We do not collect:

  • Personal Information: This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, phone number, or any other contact information.
  • Usage Data: We do not track how you interact with the App. We do not collect analytics data, such as session duration, features used, or crash reports.
  • Device Information: We do not collect any information about the device on which you are using the App, such as the device model, operating system version, or unique device identifiers.
  • Location Data: The App does not collect, use, or request any information about your geographic location.

3. Data Sharing and Transfer

Since we do not collect any user data, there is no data to share with or transfer to any third parties. We do not engage with any third-party services for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose that would involve sharing user information.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is designed to be safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. As we do not collect any data whatsoever, we do not have any information from any user, including children, to use or disclose.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect any changes in our practices, even if those changes maintain our "no data collection" stance. Any updates will be posted within the App or on our designated webpage. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please do not hesitate to contact us.
